Exercise 0: the setup

This section doesn't have any code. It is an instuction set on how to setup xilinx and use the iSim to view your results.

Mac OSX

Sorry, but at this time there is no xilix for osx. You either have to use a virtual machine to to run windows or use bootcamp.

Windows

It takes time to acquire a copy of xilinx. Here are some insctructions.

  1. go to xilinx.com
  2. go to downloads
  3. ISE Design Tools
  4. Download the "ISE Design Suite" for windows
  5. You may be required to sign-in. do it
  6. the download will take a long time. Xilix is greater than 5GB
  7. Once you have it unzip it and install
  8. The installation will take several hours
  9. It may give you an option to select what parts of xilinx to install. choose the largest one.
  10. It may ask you for a license. get one using your online xilinx account. the webpack is free and should let you do everything in this tutorial. go ahead and download the license to your computer.
  11. click the button to copy the license (the one you just downloaded) to xilinx
  12. continue on with the installation
